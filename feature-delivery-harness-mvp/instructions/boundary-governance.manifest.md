# Boundary Governance

```text
GOV.001 | MUST  | examples | use synthetic or open examples by default
GOV.002 | NEVER | storage  | store company raw code, Jira, PRs, logs, traces, screenshots, customer data
GOV.003 | MUST  | review   | require review for repo mutation, PR approval, merge, release, deploy, policy change, publication
GOV.004 | NEVER | agent    | auto merge, release, deploy, or promote candidates
GOV.005 | MUST  | report   | lead with feature status, cost, stage breakdown, waste, optimization, limitations
```
